Robot Wars

What?

  • Me and a friend built a 150g combat robot in two weeks last year
  • This year I have improved the design and built a robot I will fight in November

How?

  • Last years robot was designed in Fusion 360 and mostly 3D printed with aluminum armour
  • This year I have designed and built a four-bar lifter using PLA, with polycarbonate and aluminium armour
  • I have also improved the tracks and upgraded the electronic components
  • I have done all the design work while my friend has helped me to assemble it

Results

  • Our robot finished 4th out of 12 in the free-for-all
  • We lost two fights in the tournament, one due to our controller battery dying and the other had to be forfeited as our ESC needed replacing
  • This meant we won the loser's bracket and went undefeated in combat
