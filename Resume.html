Resume

Experience

JUNE 2023 – SEPTEMBER 2023, ELECTRICAL, CONTROL AND INSTRUMENTATION INTERN, FRAZER-NASH CONSULTANCY
I joined the Bristol EC&I North team for 13 weeks. My work involved PowerFactory modelling the grid impact of a fusion reactor, making electrical and thermal Simscape models, designing a high voltage electrical schematic, drawing single line diagrams for a power station, and writing supporting documentation for a nuclear power station.

JULY 2021/22 – SEPTEMBER 2021/22, RF ENGINEERING INTERN, SPIRIT SEMICONDUCTOR
I have completed two 9-week summer internships at Spirit Semiconductor, a start-up designing RF electronics for 5G. My work includes fabricating a diplexer through EM simulations in Microwave Office, ADS, and HFFS, designing a PCB for high voltage chip testing, figuring out and documenting how to use an old curve tracer to characterize ESD protection structures, and building a test setup for thirteen power amplifiers. I also became proficient with various electrical lab tools.

SEPTEMBER 2022 – PRESENT DAY, ELECTRICAL AND POWERTRAIN ENGINEER/ GROUP TECHNICAL LEAD, BRISTOL RACING
Bristol Racing make electric cars to compete in Greenpower racing events. My work includes battery characterisation, designing a heatsink model optimised through thermal simulations, implementation of the brake light detection circuit, design of the driver’s control holder, and fabrication of components using mills and lathes. I currently co-manage the eleven-person electrical and powertrain team.

OCTOBER 2019 – MARCH 2020, ENGINEERING EDUCATION SCHEME
I was one of six students challenged to design a lifting device to remove up to 500 kg of radioactive waste from a particle accelerator. We built a scaled version of our design over two days at Bath University. I contributed the core idea used in our final design, did stress calculations on the design, and oversaw the write-up. The project was extremely well received, earning me the Gold Industrial Cadet award.

HOME PROJECTS
Projects built in my free time include a sunrise alarm clock, a sous vide machine, two 150g combat robots, and a monitor from an old iPad screen.
