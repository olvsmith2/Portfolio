Sunrise Alarm Clock

What?

  • I designed, programmed and built an alarm clock that wakes you up by gradually turning on LEDs

How?

  • The electronics were built on a breadboard and the software was written in Arduino
  • I then used KiCAD to make a schematic and PCB
  • The enclosure was designed in Fusion 360 and 3D-printed
  • The electronic components include: a buck converter, a microphone, a real-time clock, a four digit seven segment display, four LEDs, a MOSFET, a Raspberry Pi Pico, a rotary encoder, a mechanical switch, a 12V plug, and various resistors

Results

  • The alarm works well and I use it most days
